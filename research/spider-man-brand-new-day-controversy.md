## Controversy 1: "AI Slop" Accusations

Fans immediately accused the trailer footage of being AI-generated.

**Key moments:**
- Multiple viral tweets called it "AI slop" — *"All the Spider-Man clips I seen look like AI slop. Legit looks like they just asked ChatGPT to make a Spider-Man movie."*
- Someone asked **Grok** (X's AI) if the clip was real — Grok responded it *"looks like AI-generated or fan-made footage"*
- VFX workers pushed back hard: *"This ain't AI, real people did this who probably came late home from work and couldn't say good night to their children. Have some respect and don't call real work AI, even if it's a joke or ragebait."*

## Controversy 2: Paul Rabin — Most Hated Marvel Character Coming to MCU

The trailer confirmed MJ (Zendaya) has a new boyfriend, played by **Eman Esfandi** (Ezra Bridger from *Ahsoka*). Fans believe this is **Paul Rabin**.

**Why fans hate Paul:**
- Debuted in *Amazing Spider-Man #1* (April 2022, Zeb Wells' run)
- Introduced sharing a domestic life with MJ, raising children together
- Written as a decent man (not a villain) — which made fans angrier
- Existed solely as an editorial mechanism to keep Peter and MJ apart
- Became the most despised Marvel character in years

**Fan reaction:** Clip of Peter watching MJ with her new man at a party generated massive backlash. *"Feel for you, Peter."*

## Controversy 3: Organic Web-Shooters & Body Horror Mutation

The trailer reveals Peter undergoing a **physical mutation** — growing extra arms, developing organic web-shooters, body-horror transformation.

**Historical context:**
- Sam Raimi used organic web-shooters in 2002's *Spider-Man* — fans tried to get him removed from the film
- Purists argue organic webbing removes Peter's scientific genius
- The mutation draws from controversial comic arcs: **"The Other"** (Peter dies, reborn with enhanced powers), **"The Six Arms Saga"**, and **"Man-Spider"** (1990s animated series)

**Sources:**
- [SlashFilm — Organic webbing confirmed](https://www.slashfilm.com/2126649/spider-man-brand-new-day-trailer-tom-holland-peter-parker-change-organic-webbing/)

## Controversy 5: CGI Quality Complaints

Beyond AI accusations, fans complained about the visual quality broadly.

**Reactions:**
- *"What is this CGI?"*
- *"Why is it so muted and dark?"*
- *"They can go ahead and turn the lighting up."*

Feeds into the years-long narrative about Marvel's CGI quality declining since *Quantumania* (2023) and the 2022 *New Yorker* expose on overworked VFX vendors.
